## Autonomi

Autonomi is an AI agent makes crypto effortless for everyone, especially those with little to no experience.

It acts as your personal financial autopilot, tracking your assets (trade and also sell), swapping tokens, staking funds, and optimizing yield.
built for accessibility, transparency, and trust, every action the ai takes is verifiable onchain, giving users confidence and full control. the wallet connects seamlessly with DeFi protocols, DAOs, and other ai agents to maximize your financial outcomes.

our mission is simple, make decentralized finance usable by anyone, turning complex crypto tasks into one tap and clear results.

**the ux design should make defi feel effortless, transparent, and safe.**
